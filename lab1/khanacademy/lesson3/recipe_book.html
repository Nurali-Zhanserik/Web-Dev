Cheese Sandwich

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Cheese 1 slice

Step 1: Take 2 slices of the bread

Step 2: Put one slice of cheese on a one slice of a bread

Step 3: Put the second slice of bread on top

Step 4: Eat as is or toast it and eat

Scrambled Eggs

  • Time: 5-7 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2
Salt a pinch
Oil 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Crack the eggs into a bowl

Step 2: Add a pinch of salt and mix with a fork

Step 3: Heat a pan and add oil

Step 4: Pour the eggs into the pan

Step 5: Stir gently until the eggs are cooked

Step 6: Remove from heat and serve

Plain Rice

  • Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 1 cup
Water 2 cups
Salt 1/2 teaspoon

Step 1: Rinse the rice with water

Step 2: Put rice, water, and salt into a pot

Step 3: Bring to a boil

Step 4: Lower the heat and cover with a lid

Step 5: Cook for about 15 minutes until the water is absorbed

Step 6: Turn off heat and let it sit for 5 mins

Step 7:Fluff with a fork and serve
