Trip to Niagara Falls!

Date: 24.06.2023

Visiting Niagara Falls was one of those experiences that instantly puts everything else on pause. The sound of the water alone is powerful, and standing near the edge you can feel the energy of the falls in the air. The mist, the movement, and the sheer scale of it all make you realize how small photos and videos are compared to seeing it in real life..

What made the trip truly special was how different the falls felt from every angle and at different times of the day. From calm panoramic views to the dramatic rush of water up close, each moment felt unique.
